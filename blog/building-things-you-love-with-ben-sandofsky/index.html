#29 - Building Things You Love, with Ben Sandofsky, Head of Development for Halide

halide engineering startup studio camera photography

From Pascal, to writing image processing code, if there is one word you absolutely can’t use to describe Ben Sandofsky‘s career, it would be “uneventful.” He was one of the first mobile engineers at Twitter, had a stint helping build Periscope, and ultimately ventured into unexplored territory to finish a photo app he always wanted, that later became Halide.

In this episode, I sit down with Ben to get a better understanding of the decisions he made in his career, and what were the critical turning points that made him start his own studio - Lux.
